## Legal Boundary
- Token represents economic rights only
- Underlying asset remains under Indonesian jurisdiction
- No land title transfer via blockchain

## Architecture Constraints
- Hybrid on-chain/off-chain model mandatory
- KYC always off-chain
- Cross-border whitelist required
- No permissionless DeFi

## Compliance Philosophy
- Compliance-by-design
- Role-based dual authorization
- Regulator visibility supported
