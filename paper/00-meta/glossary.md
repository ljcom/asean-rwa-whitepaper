# Glossary (Working Draft)

This glossary consolidates key terms used across the whitepaper. Definitions should remain consistent with `DOMAIN_KNOWLEDGE.md`.

- DIRE: Indonesian REIT structure under OJK.
- KIK: Collective Investment Contract (Indonesian investment structure).
- SPV: Special Purpose Vehicle used to hold assets and issue economic entitlements.
- Economic Rights Token: Token representing contractual revenue participation or debt claim; not land ownership.
- Sukuk Ijarah: Lease-based Shariah instrument backed by asset usage/rental.
- RBAC: Role-Based Access Control for privileged actions.
- Whitelist: Jurisdiction- and eligibility-aware list of permitted wallets/investors.
- NAV: Net Asset Value reference used for reporting, pricing integrity, and redemption terms (where applicable).
